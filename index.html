Victoria Liquor Stores

This visualization discovers the conditions about Victorian stores holding liquor licences.


Map

The map below gives a brief view of how liquor stores are distributed in Victoria.

Note that this map may show information slightly different from the reality, because some data is incomplete.


Category

Here the horizontal bar chart visualizes the frequency of stores in different license categories.


Trading Hours

Here the horizontal bar chart visualizes the frequency of stores trading in different times.

Note that some times are specific (e.g. 12AM), they are the latest trading hours of stores.


Maximum Capacity

Here the horizontal bar chart visualizes the frequency of stores that have different maximum capacities.


Gambling

Here the pie chart compares the number of shops that allow gambling with those don't allow.
